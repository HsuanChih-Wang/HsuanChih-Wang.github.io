--- layout: home author_profile: true title: Hsuan-Chih Wang's Home Page --- Hsuan-Chih Wang (王軒至) is currently an Associate Researcher in the Intelligent Transportation System Center at C. E. Consultants. Inc (CECI), Taipei City, Taiwan.
His research is focused on (1) CV-based Traffic Signal Operations and Control (2) traffic flow analysis through computational methodologies such as mathematical programming.
