--- layout: home author_profile: true title: Hsuan-Chih Wang's Home Page --- Hsuan-Chih Wang (王軒至) is currently an Research Assistant at Transportation Research Center, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung university, Taipei City, Taiwan. Prior to joining the current research group, he was an Associate Researcher at an engineering consultant company, and a traffic engineer at National Cheng Kung University.
His research is focused on Traffic Operations and Control through computational methodologies such as mathematical programming.
